## SPREAD 4-5: The Movie Magic
**Illustration:** Movie theater - Grandpa and Liam in matching Minion shirts, huge smiles, sharing popcorn, screen glowing

**Text:** "Like when they went to see Despicable Me 4. Grandpa knew ALL about Minions. He knew the Minions' names - Kevin, Stuart, Bob. He knew which movies they were in. He even knew Bob's teddy bear was named Tim! How did Grandpa know SO MUCH?"

## SPREAD 6-7: The Restaurant Revelation
**Illustration:** Restaurant booth - Grandpa and Liam with all Liam's favorites, Grandpa enthusiastically engaged

**Text:** "At restaurants, Grandpa didn't just let Liam order what he wanted. Grandpa KNEW - without even asking - that Liam wanted chips, that he liked his chicken tenders crispy, and that ice cream with sprinkles is the best. How did Grandpa remember every detail?"

## SPREAD 8-9: The Perfect Playtime
**Illustration:** Living room floor - Grandpa, Grandma, and Liam playing with action figures, Grandpa fully engaged

**Text:** "When they played with Toy Story figures, Grandpa knew Woody's catchphrases and Buzz's backstory. He did the voices JUST right. Grandpa didn't just play along - Grandpa knew the whole story, like he'd watched the movies as many times as Liam had."

## SPREAD 10-11: The Expert Level
**Illustration:** Cozy scene - Grandpa and Liam looking at a Scooby-Doo or Garfield book together

**Text:** "When Liam loved Scooby-Doo, Grandpa knew all about the Mystery Machine and Scooby Snacks. When Liam loved Garfield, Grandpa knew Garfield hated Mondays and loved lasagna. Grandpa didn't just know the names - he knew the DETAILS. The stuff only real fans know."

## SPREAD 12-13: Uncle Phil Scene
**Illustration:** Grandpa and Uncle Phil with toolboxes, Liam watching excitedly

**Text:** "When Grandpa and Uncle Phil came to fix things, Grandpa even knew which tools Liam thought were coolest and let him help with the safe parts. Grandpa paid attention to everything."

## SPREAD 14-15: The Big Discovery
**Illustration:** Liam having a realization moment - maybe a lightbulb or heart moment

**Text:** "Then one day, Liam figured it out. Grandpa DID know about Minions and Toy Story and Scooby-Doo. But Grandpa didn't know everything about them... until Liam loved them. Then Grandpa learned EVERYTHING. Every character. Every story. Every detail that mattered to Liam."

## SPREAD 16-17: The Love Lesson
**Illustration:** A warm moment between them

**Text:** "Because that's what Grandpa does. When Liam loves something, Grandpa doesn't just smile and nod. Grandpa watches the movies. Reads the books. Learns the characters. Goes ALL IN. Because Grandpa loves Liam."
